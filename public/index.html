About Us

We are a locally owned, and family
operated fitness facility in Campbell River!
We are open 24-hours a day, and have
over 8000sqft of co-ed fitness facility with
a women's only section. We offer
a relaxed, friendly, clean, air-conditioned
atmosphere with many classes for any
fitness level. We are pleased to offer other
services such as our Kids Zone, personal
training, free weights, strength machines,
nutritional consulting, tanning, and
supplements.

CLASSES

Take your fitness regime to the next level

We'll break your boring routine and

our wide selection, we have a class that
will be perfect for you.

Classes are included in all of our
